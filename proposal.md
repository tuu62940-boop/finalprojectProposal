Just like the original, the player will control a paddle to bounce a ball and break blocks, but instead of only moving left and right, the paddle can also move up and down. This extra movement adds a new layer of strategy and difficulty to the game, as players will need to position themselves in both axes to keep the ball in play. The game will include features such as score tracking and possibly power-ups like larger paddles or multiple balls. It will be executed using Python with a application like Pygame. This twist makes the classic gameplay more dynamic and engaging while still keeping the core mechanics familiar.
